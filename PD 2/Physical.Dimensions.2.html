Meet The Team

Lee has worked in the health and fitness sector for more than 20 years and is an experienced and successful personal trainer and nutritionist.

Holding a Bacherlors degree and post-graduate qualifications in various Sports & Exercise Sciences, Lee has worked both in the UK and overseas. He works with individual atheletes and teams, as well as major employees from blue-chip organisations such as British Airways and RBS. In the recent past he has worked with the Bloomberg Institute and the European Parliament in tackling obesity and in developing a unique set of training methods and delivery strategies that effectively reduce weight and improve fitness in a sustainable way; and with the Ministry of Defence on fitness in extreme environments.

Lee is also fiercly determined to raise the quality and standard of service in the health and fitness sector, and actively campaigns for the implementation of updated and relevant industry qualifications. He now also lectures on sustainable health and wellness to corporate, educational and medical audiences, and is a former winner of the nation 'Evolution Award'.
